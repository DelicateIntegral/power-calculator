Power Calculator

This Scale considers my power scale:

0PP: You

1PP: Peak Human

100PP: Universal Level

This is initial power expression.
All powers have potential to grow exponentially every year with the rate of 1PP worth of growth per year if you use it regularly and practice daily. This means that if you have initial power costing 25PP, after 1 year, it will be 26PP.
You can enter value greater than 100 up to 1000000 for the purpose of calculating power growth. Example: 125 value will tell you how stronger your 75 PP power would be after 50 years.
